# Social Content Creation Workflow

A streamlined workflow for maintaining a consistent social media presence.

1. **Ideation & Hook Generation**
   - Call `agent-media-copywriter` to generate a batch of content ideas for the week.

2. **Visual Production**
   - Call `agent-media-designer` to create matching visuals or avatars for each content piece.

3. **Managerial Oversight**
   - Call `agent-media-manager` to schedule the posts across all platforms at optimal times.
